# 1D sign conventions for rope problems

Pick **one** positive direction along motion for each independent coordinate, or one for “along the rope” and express all accelerations relative to it.

## Common choice

- Take **positive** in the direction the **heavier** side would accelerate if released, or along the **haul** direction—either is fine if consistent.

## Checklist

- Same sign for **both** sides of \(F = ma\).
- Tension always **pulls** toward the rope; never flip sign arbitrarily between ends of one ideal massless segment.

Document your choice in one sentence at the start of a solution.
